About Us

Botanical Battery is a company that specializes in developing innovative technologies for sustainable energy production and storage.

Our Technology

Our patented Plant Microbial Fuel Cell (PMFC) technology uses Spider plant to generate electricity through the natural metabolic processes of microorganisms in the soil around the plant roots. Our PMFCs are efficient, scalable, and environmentally friendly, and can be used in a variety of applications, from powering small devices to providing backup power for homes and businesses.
